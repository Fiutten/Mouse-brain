# Digital-Twin Claim Audit

- Passed claims: `1`
- Blocked claims: `3`

## Audited Claims

| Claim | Passed | Allowed wording | Blocked wording |
|---|---:|---|---|
| `whole_brain_digital_twin` | `False` |  | partial mouse-brain model or benchmark; not a complete whole-brain twin |
| `single_neuron_resolution_connectivity` | `True` | single-neuron connectivity model |  |
| `mechanistic_identifiability` | `False` |  | predictive or structure-associated result, not causal mechanism |
| `behavioral_digital_twin` | `False` |  | task-related neural dynamics with limited behavioral decoding |

## Interpretation

Digital-twin wording is accepted only when the corresponding evidence gates pass. Prediction, BOLD correlation, and scale do not substitute for independent structure, causal, or reproducibility evidence.
