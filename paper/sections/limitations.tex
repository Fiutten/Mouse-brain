\section{Limitations}

The current MICRONS result is observational. It does not establish causality,
synaptic necessity, or behavioral function. The analysis is also local to the
available MICRONS cortical volume and cannot be generalized to the whole mouse
brain without additional evidence.

The Allen VBN analysis is used as a negative-control layer and currently
analyzes a preselected subset rather than every available session in the full
public resource. Expanding Allen is possible, but it is not required for the
current MICRONS-centered claim.

The Sensorium official baseline is a bounded local training/evaluation artifact.
It proves that the official ecosystem can be executed inside MouseBrainBench,
but it is not a leaderboard-equivalent or state-of-the-art Sensorium result.

The framework does not simulate consciousness, behavior, or a complete mouse
brain. It audits partial digital models and their claims.
