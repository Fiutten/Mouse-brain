\section*{Data and code availability}
The source code, fixed configurations, acquisition scripts, and machine-readable
results are maintained in the MouseBrainBench repository. Raw public data are
not redistributed. The final archived release, repository URL, and DOI will be
inserted after acceptance of the experimental freeze used for submission.
MICRONS queries require acceptance of the CAVE terms and a personal access
token; the queried materialization, tables, offsets, and output checksums are
recorded by the acquisition scripts.
